== Description ==

[Merchoo](https://merchoo.com/) is a free plugin that lets you sell customized merchandise on your website. Merchoo does the heavy lifting and you earn money when items sell.

Merchoo is 100% free. There are no hidden costs and no minimum orders. Your visitors can buy as little or as much as they want. Merchoo lets you easily sell merchandise by taking care of the following, so you don't have to:

* Product Creation
* Inventory
* Payment Processing
* Shipping & Handling
* Customer Service

After you install the plugin, simply [create an account](https://merchoo.com/) to get your Merchoo account number. Update your plugin settings with this number and you're good to go.

[Login](https://merchoo.com/login.php) to your Merchoo account to create designs and check your earnings.




== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.


1. Upload the `Merchoo` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Go to Merchoo 'Settings' to enter your Merchoo account number and choose button colors.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What is Merchoo? =

Merchoo is a free plugin that lets you sell customized merchandise on your website. It's free and easy.

= How does Merchoo work? =

Once you install our plugin and create an account at [Merchoo.com](https://merchoo.com/), all that's left is to create designs you want to sell with our easy-to-use design tool. All your designs are automatically created into different products that show in your store. When a product sells, you earn a commission.

= How much does it cost? =

Merchoo is completely free. Create a store and start selling with no cost.

= How much commission do I earn? =

You will earn 17% commission from every sale in your Merchoo store.

= What type of merchandise can I sell? =

You can sell T-shirts, Hoodies, Tote bags, Ladies Tees, Long sleeve shirts, children's shirts and more!

= How do I get a Merchoo account number? =

[Create an account](https://merchoo.com/) and follow the instructions to get your account number.

= Have more questions? =

Visit [Merchoo.com](https://merchoo.com/) to learn more.
